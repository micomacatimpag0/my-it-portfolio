About Us

Protecting Eastern Samar's Natural Heritage

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) in Borongan City, Eastern Samar is the primary government agency responsible for the conservation, management, development, and proper use of the province's environment and natural resources.

Under the leadership of our dedicated officials, we work tirelessly to ensure equitable sharing of benefits derived from natural resources for the welfare of present and future generations of Filipinos.

Our Mission

To mobilize our citizenry in protecting, conserving, and managing the environment and natural resources for the present and future generations.

Our Vision

A nation enjoying and sustaining its natural resources and a clean and healthy environment.

Our Mandate

Executive Order No. 192 mandates DENR to be the primary agency responsible for conservation, management, development, and proper use of the country's environment and natural resources.

Our Objectives

Strategic Goals for Environmental Stewardship

Guided by our mandate, we pursue these key objectives to ensure sustainable management of Eastern Samar's natural resources.

Sustainability

Assure the availability and sustainability of the country's natural resources through judicious use and systematic restoration.

Productivity

Increase the productivity of natural resources to meet the demands of a growing population.

Development

Enhance the contribution of natural resources for achieving national economic and social development.

Equitable Access

Promote equitable access to natural resources by the different sectors of the population.

Conservation

Conserve terrestrial and marine areas representative of Philippine natural and cultural heritage.
